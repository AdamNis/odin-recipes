Instant Pot® Sweet Baby Back Ribs

Description

This Delicious dish of baby back ribs, is tasty, easy to make, easy to clean and all around a great experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 racks baby back ribs
  • 1/4 cup dark brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp garlic salt
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper,or to taste(optional)
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 12 fluid ounces root beer
  • 2 tbsp apple cidar vinegar
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke flavoring
  • 1 cup barbecue sauce
